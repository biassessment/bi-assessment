Dear Participant,

Effectively using Business Intelligence (BI) tools is becoming increasingly important to derive insights from the huge amount of collected data and make better business decisions. However, many companies still do not leverage the full potential of these tools. Therefore, this research project, running at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Institute of Information Systems and Marketing (Research Group Information Systems and Service Design, Prof. Dr. Alexander Mädche), aims to assess your current usage of BI tools and identify areas for improvement.

If you are a user of BI tools, we would like to invite you to take part in the project by completing a short survey which will just take about 10 minutes. You can be assured that all of your responses will be treated absolutely confidentially. The survey is completely anonymous and only aggregate results will be published. Your honest response to each question is extremely important to the outcome of this project. You can take breaks at any time and continue later. The survey saves your answers and you can resume where you left off.

If you like, we offer to give you feedback with recommendations via a dedicated feedback report. To receive the report, please enter your email address on the last page of the survey.

If you have any questions regarding this study, please do not hesitate to contact us. 

 

Thank you for your consideration and support,
